About UKHUSELO SUPPLIER PORTAL

UKHUSELO SUPPLIER PORTAL is a controlled marketplace that connects members with verified security and related suppliers, combining structured procurement, compliance verification, and escrow-backed payments.

Structured Categories

Requests are captured against standardised service and product categories, making it easy to brief and compare suppliers.

Typical categories include uniforms and PPE, SIM cards and connectivity, access control systems, patrol monitoring, two-way radios, financial assistance, and insurance solutions.

Competitive Bidding

Real-time job alerts notify eligible suppliers when a new request matches their profile. Suppliers respond with structured quotations.

The platform supports transparent bid comparison and digital award confirmation so members can select the best-value offer with confidence.

Compliance & Verification

TAPSOSA centralises supplier and member verification, including regulatory documents, credentials, and eligibility checks.

Expiry alerts and trade controls help ensure only compliant, up-to-date parties can transact on the platform.

Secure Payments & Escrow

An integrated payment gateway with escrow functionality protects both members and suppliers during the transaction.

Fees are deducted automatically, funds are released on completion or milestones, and a full audit trail supports financial reporting and reconciliation.

Strategic Value

By standardising procurement and enforcing compliance, UKHUSELO SUPPLIER PORTAL reduces risk for members and ensures supplier credibility.

The platform creates a transparent trading environment with measurable transactional revenue that strengthens governance and accountability.
