Pricing & Fees

UKHUSELO SUPPLIER PORTAL uses a simple, transaction-based pricing model. A platform fee is applied to each payment, and the net payout is released to the supplier once work is completed.

Platform Fee

The standard UKHUSELO SUPPLIER PORTAL fee is 7% of the member’s payment. This fee supports platform operations, compliance checks, secure escrow, and transaction monitoring.

The remaining 93% is available as the supplier’s gross payout, subject to any tax or other charges applicable on their side.

Example Scenario

If a member approves a payment of R100,000 for a contract, UKHUSELO SUPPLIER PORTAL applies a 7% fee (R7,000) and the supplier’s gross payout is R93,000.

The fee is calculated automatically within the escrow process, so both parties always see the breakdown before approval.

Who Pays What

The member funds the full contract amount into escrow. The platform fee is deducted as part of the transaction, and net funds are released to the supplier on completion or agreed milestones.

This ensures clarity for members on total cost, and predictability for suppliers on expected net receipts.

Value for Members

The platform fee includes access to competitive bidding, compliance checks, audit trails, and dispute reduction, helping members de-risk procurement decisions.

Members benefit from transparent supplier comparisons and structured workflows without needing to build this capability internally.

Value for Suppliers

Suppliers gain access to qualified, verified demand, faster award decisions, and escrow-backed payments that reduce payment risk.

A single, predictable fee structure makes it easier to price contracts and forecast revenue.
